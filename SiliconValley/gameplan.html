  • I will begin by looking at the history of Silicon Valley in the context of its two major "rushes": Gold in 1849 and electronics manufacturing one hundred years later. This will naturally transition into a sketch of the state of the region today.
  • Next, I'll provide a brief overview of the different technologies that have been important in Silicon Valley's development. Most notable among these are the transistor, the personal computer, and social media, all of which use the area's namesake silicon-based technology.
  • Finally, I'll round things off with musings on the future of Silicon Valley, a subject of much discussion and debate among technocrats, businesspeople, and scientists these days.
