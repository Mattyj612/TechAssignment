About This Website




In the spirit of a true Silicon Valley entrepreneur, I decided to design my own website for this project. I deliberately avoided the cookie-cutter website models in favour of an original design which, though certainly not the most impressive internet page in existence, is entirely my own. This means that, on top of the actual content of this website, I've written an estimated 2000 lines of computer code which specify every single minute detail from the colour of this text to the distance between adjacent navigation bars.

Having never forayed into web design before, this was a challenging and often frustrating experience. It gave me a newfound appreciation for the work that goes on in those new-wave, progressive, avant-garde offices; those Hawaiian shirted, sandle-clad youngsters are damn smart.

It's worth noting that the laptop on which this website was designed was manufactured by three Silicon Valley-associated brands: HP produced the physical framework, Intel manufactured the microprocessors, and Microsoft designed the operating system.
