Conclusion




It takes immense courage, ambition, and confidence to leap head-first into San Francisco Bay. The area went from fruitful farmland to technological haven and entrepreneurial incubator in the span of a century, led by brave risk-takers who saw their chance and took it. The history of 20th century electronics and the rise of the internet are inextricably linked to the story of Silicon Valley, a remarkable feat when considering the ubiquity and overwhelming universality of computers, television, and radio. Companies in Silicon Valley are serving billions of people worldwide and are constantly finding creative and innovative new ways to reinvent themselves and keep with the times.

At the beginning of this website, we sought to discover how a small inlet region of beaches surrounded by mountains was able to become the Mecca of technology, and we've come a long way to understanding it.. With the right mix of colorful personalities — Terman, Shockley, and Jobs, to name a few (though I didn't much discuss the latter due to time concerns) — innovative ideas — the transistor, the personal computer, and social media — and probably a lot of luck, Silicon Valley has become a center of business, technology, and in some sense culture, and remains the defining symbol of modern innovation.


  • Matthew Jordan, 2014. Arts & Science 3B03
