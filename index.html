Welcome To Silicon Valley!




You've founded your very first start-up and have decided to move to the international entrepreneurial hub: Silicon Valley. You'll be joining thousands of other young people looking to make waves in the technological big leagues and are hoping you can set yourself apart from the brilliant, eccentric crowd. Located in the scenic San Francisco Bay Area, Silicon Valley is home to the world's largest electronics corporations and wealthiest people. But how did this palm-treed fruit farmer's haven become the de facto Mecca of 20th and 21st century technology? That's precisely what this website seeks to uncover.
